Vegas Projection

Concepts

  • Want to use offensive players from teams with high team totals. we want to target defenses facing teams with low totals.
    • QB
      • Target underdog QB’s in games with high team totals and close spreads, ideally, but not necessarily, playing at home.
      • Avoid QB’s in division match-ups, particularly later in the season.
      • Avoid QB’s and Pass Catchers in games with spreads over -8 that are likely to develop into blowouts.
    • RB - Lines are more useful for running backs, teams projected to score 30 or more points in a game rush for 14% more than teams projected at 21 and 33% more than teams at 14 or less.
      • Target RB’s on heavily favored teams playing at home.
      • Take advantage of RB’s filling in for injured starters.
      • In cash games try to use RB’s that are not affected as much by game flows. This generally means spending up for consistent backs.
    • WR
      • Look for games with high totals and close spreads of 4 points or less
      • Don’t shy away from choosing WR’s on underdogs particularly when they are playing at home.
      • When facing an elite CB, consider pivoting to a team’s WR2 as a value play, particularly if the CB shadows WR1’s and the WR2 is a viable stand alone WR
    • K & DST
      • Chose defenses from favored teams playing at home in games with low totals. Ideally, teams should be heavily favored with spreads at -7 or above.
      • Whenever possible, pair up DST with the Kicker to take advantage of the correlation between the two positions.
  • WE can make assumptions regarding how many tds or fgs. Start of year use stats from last year, after week 8 should be using solely this years stats.
    • Passing TDs = Total Points / 7 * % of tds over last 8-16 games
    • WR/TE TDs = Passing Tds * % of td caches over last 8-16 games
  • Vegas lines allow us to qunatify the effect and do it in a really efficient way so we can spend precious research time elsewhere.
  • Vegas lines is a really accurate way to figure out the most likley thing thats going to happen and use that to win in cash games. In tournaments use the lines as a tool to predict player utilization.
  • Weather is not projected well in vegas lines. Lines are out before weather forcast.
